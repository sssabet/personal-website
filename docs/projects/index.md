# Projects

Here you can find information about my various projects and research work.

## Recent Projects

### [HITAI: AI-Powered Combat Sports Intelligence Platform](2025-01-27-hitai-launch.md)
**January 2025** - As Co-founder & CTO, I'm excited to announce the launch of HITAI, the first all-in-one combat sports analytics platform. Our AI-powered solution turns raw fight footage into real-time insights using only video and computer vision - no sensors or wearables required.

### [VR Soccer: XR + AI Fan Engagement Platform](2025-01-27-vr-soccer-xr-ai.md)
**January 2025** - Innovative XR + AI application designed to revolutionize fan engagement during live soccer games. Combines computer vision, AI-powered player detection, and 3D visualization to show real-time player positions on an interactive 3D map.

### [SmartCrop: AI-Based Cropping of Soccer Videos](2024-09-30-smart-crop-soccer.md)
**September 2024** - AI-based system designed to enhance the presentation of soccer videos by intelligently identifying points of interest within each frame. Now in production with Forzasys, actively used by Swedish and Norwegian soccer clubs.

### [SmartCrop-H: AI-Based Cropping for Ice Hockey](2024-10-10-smart-crop-hockey.md)
**October 2024** - Advanced AI-based video cropping solution tailored specifically for ice hockey. Now in production with Forzasys and used by the Swedish Hockey League (SHL).
