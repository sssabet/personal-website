    HITAI: AI-Powered Combat Sports Intelligence Platform

    Demo Video

    Overview

    HITAI is the first all-in-one combat sports analytics platform that revolutionizes how athletes train and promoters enhance events. As the Co-founder & CTO of HITAI, I'm excited to announce the launch of our AI-powered fight intelligence platform that turns raw fight footage into real-time insights using only video and computer vision - no sensors or wearables required.

    Our platform leverages cutting-edge computer vision and AI technologies to provide comprehensive analytics for combat sports, helping athletes train smarter, coaches make data-driven decisions, and promoters engage fans like never before.

    The Problem

    Combat sports have traditionally relied on intuition and manual observation for training and analysis. Athletes and coaches lack objective data to measure progress, track performance metrics, and optimize training routines. Promoters struggle to engage audiences with meaningful statistics and insights during live events.

    Traditional solutions require expensive sensors, wearables, or manual video analysis, making them inaccessible to most gyms and athletes. There's a clear need for an automated, AI-driven solution that can extract valuable insights from existing video footage.

    The Solution: AI-Powered Combat Sports Analytics

    HITAI solves these challenges by providing a plug-and-play AI training station that requires only a smartphone or camera feed. Our platform automatically tracks:

    • Strikes, power, and movement in real-time
    • Performance metrics across training sessions
    • Comparative analytics between athletes and sessions
    • Automated content generation for promoters and broadcasters

    Key Features:

    For Athletes & Coaches:

    • Smart Training Analytics: Track strikes, power, and movement with just your phone
    • Progress Monitoring: Replace intuition with data-driven decision making
    • Performance Comparison: Analyze improvements over time and compare with training partners

    For Gyms & Teams:

    • Multi-Athlete Dashboards: Comprehensive team analytics and individual performance tracking
    • Training Optimization: Identify areas for improvement and track team progress
    • Resource Management: Optimize training schedules based on performance data

    For Promoters & Broadcasters:

    • Real-Time Fight Statistics: Live analytics during events
    • Automated Content Generation: Generate engaging content and statistics automatically
    • Visual Overlays: Add fight stats, life bars, and visual enhancements to live broadcasts

    Technical Innovation

    HITAI's core technology is built on advanced computer vision algorithms that can:

    • Detect and track multiple athletes simultaneously
    • Analyze movement patterns and strike effectiveness
    • Process video in real-time for live event coverage
    • Generate insights without requiring any additional hardware

    Our platform is designed to work with existing video equipment, making it accessible to gyms, teams, and promoters of all sizes.

    Company Background

    HITAI was founded with over 20 years of hands-on fight experience and Olympic-level coaching expertise. Our team combines deep knowledge of combat sports with cutting-edge AI technology, ensuring that our platform is designed by and for the combat sports world.

    As the Co-founder & CTO, I bring my expertise in developing AI systems for VR training and managing cloud-based data solutions from my work at Forzasys AS and Simula Research Lab. This experience has been crucial in building robust, scalable AI infrastructure for real-time combat sports analytics.

    Market Impact

    HITAI is positioned to revolutionize the combat sports industry by:

    • Democratizing access to advanced analytics for all levels of athletes
    • Enhancing training effectiveness through data-driven insights
    • Improving fan engagement with real-time statistics and content
    • Creating new revenue streams for promoters and content creators

    Launch and Availability

    HITAI is now available for early access and beta testing. We're actively seeking:

    • Beta testers to help shape the platform
    • Partnerships with gyms, teams, and promoters
    • Feedback from the combat sports community

    Conclusion

    HITAI represents the next evolution in combat sports technology. By combining AI-powered analytics with deep domain expertise, we're creating a platform that will help athletes train smarter, coaches make better decisions, and promoters engage fans in entirely new ways.

    This isn't just technology - it's the next round for combat sports. Join us in revolutionizing how the world trains, competes, and experiences combat sports.
